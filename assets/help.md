# Gym Royale Help & Support

Welcome to Gym Royale! This guide will help you get started, understand the app features, and troubleshoot common issues.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Sign In / Sign Up**  
   Create an account to start tracking your workouts and earning points on the leaderboard.

2. **Home / Main Tab**  
   - View the leaderboard and see how you rank compared to other users.  
   - Track your workouts using the “Workouts” section.  
   - Use the **Check In at Gym** button to earn streak points when you are near the gym.

---

## Logging Workouts

1. Tap the **"+"** floating action button in the **Workouts** section.  
2. Fill out the form:
   - **Workout Name** – Give your workout a name.  
   - **Activity Type** – Choose **Lift** or **Cardio**.  
   - **Lift workouts** – Enter Weight, Sets, and Reps.  
   - **Cardio workouts** – Enter Duration in minutes.  
3. Press **Save**. Your workout is automatically added to your log.

> Workouts are synced in real-time. You can see updates immediately across devices.

---

## Editing & Deleting Workouts

- Tap the **more options icon (⋮)** on a workout card.  
- Select **Edit** to open the workout form pre-filled with existing values.  
- Make your changes and save.  
- Select **Delete** to remove a workout permanently.

---

## Gym Check-Ins

- Use the **Check In at Gym** button to earn points.  
- You must be within ~200 meters of the gym to check in (location permissions required).  
- You can only check in **once per day**.  
- Missing a day will **reset your streak**.

> **Tips:**  
> - Enable location permissions for accurate check-ins.  
> - Check-ins contribute to your leaderboard points.

---

## Leaderboard

- Points are awarded based on check-ins and workouts.  
- Streaks increase the points earned per check-in.  
- Leaderboard updates in real-time so you can see your rank immediately.  
- Tapping your name may show your stats (future feature).

## Tips & Troubleshooting

- **Location Permissions:** Make sure Gym Royale has location access for check-ins.  
- **Streaks Reset:** If you miss a day, your streak will reset to 0.  
- **Sync Issues:** All data is stored in Firebase. Ensure you have an internet connection.  
- **Editing Workouts:** Only workouts added with the "+" button can currently be edited.
